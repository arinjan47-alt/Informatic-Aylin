    • Somtam Thai

      Somtam, Thailand’s famous green papaya salad, is a dish that proves simple ingredients can create unforgettable flavor. Made with shredded green papaya, fresh lime, fish sauce, chilies, tomatoes, and peanuts, Somtam hits the perfect balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy—a combination that keeps people coming back for more.

    • Somtam Para

      Originating from Thailand’s Isan region, Somtam is more than street food; it’s a cultural staple enjoyed everywhere from family kitchens to busy markets. Whether you love it mild or fiery, traditional or with salted crab or fermented fish, there’s a Somtam style for every palate. Light, refreshing, and bursting with personality, Somtam is the kind of dish that instantly transports you to the heart of Thailand. One bite, and you’ll understand why it’s adored around the world.
